Volunteer

Creps2Africa are always looking for people to get involved and help our mission become a reality. There are many ways to get involved, such as joining the Creps2Africa team. Our team consists of people that have a passion to leave a positive impact on the world and want to help the less fortunate.

Shoe Donations

We want you to help us put shoes on the children's feet by sending us your precious outgrown shoes.

  • Shoes must have no holes
  • Shoes must have laces
  • Shoes must not be out outworn
  • Shoes must be clean
  • Shoes must have both pairs

    • Donations

    Our shoes travel across the world to find children in need of them and this trip can be expensive. With your financial support, it makes it an easier job for us to send your dear shoes to where they need to be.
