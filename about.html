Who we are

My name is Johnfa Koke and I’m the creator of Creps2Africa. Creps2Africa is a non-profit organisation which sends outgrown shoes to schools, clubs & orphanages in Africa.

Shoes are part of every humans basic needs. However, many people especially in Africa are deprived of their basic needs. Can you imagine going the whole day without wearing shoes whilst doing your daily tasks? I can’t either, which is why I want to give people a helping hand. Everyone deserves to be wearing a pair of shoes.

There are over 300 million barefoot children worldwide whom are in danger to infections, such as hookworms. Other injuries to the feet can lead to ulcers and other conditions which are left untreated. Many children are not permitted to go school because they don't own a pair of shoes.

Help us, help them.

We’re working on a project where we’ve planned to send shoes to 4 different countries, which are DR Congo, Nigeria, Ghana & Cameroon.
